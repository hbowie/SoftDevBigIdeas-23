No substitutes for analysis and design

Prototypes and prototyping are not substitutes for analysis and design, not excuses for sloppy thinking.

– Larry Constantine and Lucy Lockwood, Software for Use: A Practical Guide to the Models and Methods of Usage-Centered Design, 1999
